TamerClaw Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

TamerClaw ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Voice Data: When you use voice features, audio is recorded temporarily to send to AI agents for processing. Voice recordings are not stored permanently on our servers.

Chat Messages: Text and media messages you send to AI agents are processed to generate responses. Conversation history is stored to enable session resume functionality.

Device Information: We collect basic device information (OS version, device type) for app compatibility and crash reporting.

Biometric Data: Biometric authentication (fingerprint/Face ID) is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave. We never access or store biometric data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI agent communication services
  • To enable voice-to-voice conversations with AI agents
  • To maintain conversation history for session resume
  • To send push notifications for agent replies
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using encrypted storage on your device and secure server connections (HTTPS/TLS). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AI Language Models: To generate agent responses (text content is processed but not used for training)
  • Text-to-Speech Services: To convert agent text responses to voice audio

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data as necessary to provide the app's core functionality (e.g., sending messages to AI processing services).

7. Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your conversation data
  • Export your conversation history
  • Opt out of push notifications
  • Delete your account and all associated data

8. Children's Privacy

TamerClaw is designed to be used by individuals of all ages, including children with speech disabilities, under the supervision of a parent or caregiver. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
